Business Entry Graph

This graph shows the total number of business entries in different states from 2020 to 2023. The data represents the trend of new businesses being established over time, providing insights into economic growth and stability. Notably, there is a steep decrease after the COVID years, indicating a slowdown in the formation of new businesses following the initial pandemic period.

Business Exit Graph

This graph represents the total number of business exits in different states from 2020 to 2023. It highlights the rate of businesses closing down during these years, which can be a reflection of economic challenges or market shifts. In conjunction with the previous chart, this shows a steep increase in businesses closing down after the COVID years, illustrating a significant shift in the business landscape following the pandemic.
